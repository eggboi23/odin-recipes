Summer Sweet Smoothies

Packed full of sweet summer fruity goodness. Great for a breakfast or lunch when you're on the go.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cranberry juice
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup watermelon chunks
  • 1 banana
  • 2 fresh figs

Directions

  1. Process the cranberry juice, strawberries, blueberries, watermelon, banana, and figs in a blender until smooth and creamy. Enjoy immediately or keep cool in refrigerator.
